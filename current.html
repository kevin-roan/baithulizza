WELCOME TO BAITHUL IZZA ARTS & SCIENCE COLLEGE

BaithulIzza Arts & Science College has already earned a niche for itself among institutions of higher learning in Kerala. Away from the din and bustle of a great city and yet easily accessible to all its amenities, this institution is ideally located on the Padanilam-Narikkuni Road. With great visionaries at the apex of its administrative hierarchy and eminent scholars on the teaching staff, BaithulIzza Arts and Science College set a fast pace of development. With the cream of students from all over Kerala converging to this premier institution, the sprawling campus of Baithul Izza arts and Science College is an enviable confluence of excellence maintaining an ideal ambience and striking a delicate balance between curricular and co-curricular activities.

The institution has provided state of the art facilitiesin all the disciplines to utilize the potential of the students in a creative manner and to serve the public in a better way.

The institution aims at the formation of socially conscious leaders who are intellectually competent, spiritually enlightened, morally upright, psychologically integrated and physically healthy.
